Here is a table list with the 5 most popular Greek Movies. This table is based on IMDb's database.

Title Year Scenario Cast
The Dragon (Ο Δράκος) 1956 A peaceful, frightened little man is mistakenly identified as "the dragon", a notorious criminal at large. He briefly and reluctantly rules the underworld until they realize their mistake. Dinos Iliopoulos, Margarita Papageorgiou, Giannis Argyris, Thanasis Vengos
Το ξύλο βγήκε από τον παράδεισο 1959 A young idealist scholar at an all-girls boarding school comes face to face with the institution's privileged students. Will they learn a thing or two about respect? Can a little discipline pave the way for a fresh whirlwind romance? Aliki Vougiouklaki, Dimitris Papamichael, Hristos Tsaganeas, Dionysis Papagiannopoulos
Red Lanterns (Τα κόκκινα φανάρια) 1963 The inextricably intertwined stories of five women who work as prostitutes just before the ban on brothels in Troumba. Jenny Karezi, Giorgos Foundas, Dimitris Papamichael, Manos Katrakis
Aleksis Zorbas (Αλέξης Ζορμπάς) 1964 An uptight English writer travelling to Crete, on a matter of business, finds his life changed forever when he meets the gregarious Alexis Zorba. Anthony Quinn, Alan Bates, Irene Papas, Lila Kedrova
Ρεμπέτικο (Rebetiko) 1983 The story of a group of Rembetes, singers and musicians of the Greek equivalent to the blues, in the early decades of the 20th century, seen through the eyes of a young female singer. Sotiria Leonardou, Nikos Kalogeropoulos, Michalis Maniatis, Themis Bazaka
